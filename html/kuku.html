Kuku Kube

Test your visual perception and speed in Kuku Kube! Your goal is to spot the square with a slightly different color from the rest as quickly as possible. The game gets progressively harder, challenging your focus and reflexes. Can you beat your own best time and become a master at spotting subtle differences?
